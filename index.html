    OUR STORY

    Our Journey: Crafting Culinary
    Excellence Since 1927

    Signature Cuisine is more than just a restaurant; it's a tale of passion, tradition, and exceptional flavors that began in the heart of Sri Lanka. Our story starts on Flower Road, Colombo 07, where a small family eatery opened its doors in 1927.

    From humble beginnings, our founders poured their hearts into every dish they served, and word quickly spread about our delectable cuisine. Signature Cuisine became a local favorite, cherished for its authentic Sri Lankan flavors and warm hospitality.

    Over the decades, we've grown and evolved, always staying true to our roots. Today, we are proud to have 11 branches across the country, each one a testament.

    Our commitment to quality is unwavering. We source the finest ingredients, handpicked from local farms and markets, ensuring that every dish we create carries the essence of Sri Lanka's rich culinary heritage...
